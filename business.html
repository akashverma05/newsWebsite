GST collections cross ₹ 1 lakh crore in Oct for first time in 8 months

GST collections in October stood at over ₹ 1.05 lakh crore, crossing for the first time ₹ 1 lakh crore mark since February this year, the finance ministry said on Sunday. The gross GST revenue collected in the month of October 2020 is ₹ 1,05,155 crore of which CGST is ₹ 19,193 crore, SGST is ₹ 5,411 crore, IGST is ₹ 52,540 crore (including ₹ 23,375 crore collected on import of goods) and cess is ₹ 8,011 crore (including ₹ 932 crore collected on import of goods), the ministry said in a statement.
